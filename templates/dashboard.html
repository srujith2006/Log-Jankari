ℹ️ About This Platform

This platform helps rescue teams and community members identify missing survivors. By providing information about unidentified survivors, you're helping save lives and reunite families. Your contributions are valuable and help the rescue operation succeed.

🎯 How to Help

  1. Browse unidentified survivors on the home page
  2. If you recognize someone, click "Help" or "Provide Information"
  3. Fill in their details (name, contact information, etc.)
  4. Submit the information - it will help with the rescue efforts
  5. Check your profile to see your contributions
